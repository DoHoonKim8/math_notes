Definition
- linear transformation $T: V \rightarrow W$ and if $T$ is 
	- one to one(injective),
	- onto(surjective),
	then $T$ is called isomorphism and $V, W$ are isomorphic

- isomorphism is an equivalence relation
	- reflexive, symmetric, transitive하다는 뜻

- matrix of T relative to the ordered bases $\mathcal{B}, \mathcal{B'}$

Theorem.
Every $n$-dimensional vector space over field $F$ is isomorphic to $F^n$

주의할 점은, 이 때 대응이 linear transformation이고, 1-1, onto임을 보여야 한다.
